Support FAQ

Click on a question below to reveal the answer.

Can I invite existing associates to the InitHub community?

Yes, you can invite up to 5 people and they will be automatically approved for InitHub access. Click "Collaboration" on top navigation, then click "Invite" on the left side navigation.

How do I change my password?

Click "Profile" on top navigation, then click "Update Password"

How do I remove my Services from my profile?

Click "Profile" on top navigation, then click "Services". On this form you can remove services by clicking the "X" to the right of the Service name.

How do I remove my Interests from my profile?

Click "Profile" on top navigation, then click "Interests". On this form you can remove Interests by clicking the "X" to the right of the Interest name.

How do I manage email subscriptions

Click "Profile" on top navigation, then click "Subscriptions". On this form you can add and remove the emails you will receive.
